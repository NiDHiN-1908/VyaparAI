# Company Knowledge

## Business Details
- **Business Name**: Green Haven Nursery
- **Brand Identity**: Premium eco-friendly plants, organic seeds, and custom gardening supplies.
- **Company Overview**: Green Haven Nursery is India's leading green-tech nursery. We specialize in premium indoor air-purifying plants, exotic bonsai, rose cultivars, and sustainable urban gardening essentials.
- **Mission**: To bring nature into urban homes, promote organic farming practices, and deliver high-quality foliage with carbon-neutral packaging.
- **Vision**: To be India's most trusted online destination for premium indoor plants and organic garden care products.
- **Store History**: Founded in 2018 as a small family-run greenhouse in Delhi NCR, Green Haven Nursery has grown into a major horticultural brand serving over 50,000 satisfied home gardeners across the country.
- **Certifications**: ISO 9001:2015 certified operations; all fertilizers and organic feeds are certified under India's National Programme for Organic Production (NPOP).
- **Trust Indicators**: 4.8/5 average customer rating on Google Maps (based on 2,200+ user reviews); featured in the "Indian Green Spaces Initiative 2025".
- **Business Achievements**: Awarded "Best Sustainable Urban Forestry Startup" at the India Green Summit 2024.
