# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

## General Gardening & Plant Care FAQs
- **Q: How often should I water my indoor plants?**
  - **A**: Most indoor air-purifying plants (like the Snake Plant or Fiddle Leaf Fig) should be watered only when the top 2 inches of soil feel dry to the touch (usually once every 7 to 10 days). Overwatering is the most common cause of root rot.
