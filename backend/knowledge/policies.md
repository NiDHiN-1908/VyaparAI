# Policies

## Shipping and Delivery Policy
- **Processing Time**: All orders are processed within 12 to 24 hours of confirmation.
- **Packaging**: Plants are shipped in custom, highly ventilated, eco-friendly modular containers to prevent soil spillage and plant stress.
- **Shipping Rates**: 
  - Standard ground shipping is flat Rs. 99 for orders under Rs. 999.
  - Shipping is 100% free for all orders of Rs. 999 and above.
- **Estimated Delivery Times**:
  - Gurugram and Delhi NCR: Same-day or next-day delivery (Express).
  - Metro cities (Mumbai, Bengaluru, Chennai, Kolkata): 2 to 3 business days.
  - Rest of India: 4 to 6 business days.

## Return and Refund Policy
- **7-Day Green Guarantee**: If your plant arrives damaged, dry, or in an unhealthy condition, we offer a 100% free replacement or full refund. Customers must share a photo of the damaged plant within 7 days of delivery.
- **Refund Process**: Approved refunds are processed within 24 to 48 hours to the customer's original payment method (UPI/Card).
- **Return Shipping**: For non-plant items (pots, tools), we arrange a free doorstep pickup. Live plants do not need to be shipped back to us; the customer can keep or compost them.

## Exchange Policy
- **Eligible Items**: Non-living products such as ceramic pots, watering cans, and gardening tools can be exchanged within 10 days of delivery if they are unused and in original packaging.
- **Non-Exchangeable Items**: Custom soil mixes, open fertilizer packets, and discounted sale items are not eligible for exchange.

## Warranty Information
- **Gardening Equipment & Tools**: All metal tools, watering cans, and automatic drip kits carry a 30-day warranty against manufacturing defects.
- **Warranty Claims**: Send a video of the defect to support@greenhavennursery.in or message us on WhatsApp with your Order ID.
