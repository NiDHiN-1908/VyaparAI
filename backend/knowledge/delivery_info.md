# Delivery Information

## Coverage Areas
- **Same-Day Express Delivery**: Available exclusively in Gurugram, Delhi, Noida, Greater Noida, and Ghaziabad. Orders must be placed before 12:00 PM IST to qualify for same-day delivery.
- **Pan-India Standard Delivery**: Covers all tier-1, tier-2, and tier-3 cities across India via top logistics partners (Delhivery, BlueDart, Xpressbees).
- **Non-Serviceable Areas**: We currently do not deliver live plants to international locations or extremely remote islands (like Andaman & Nicobar or Lakshadweep) due to transit time limitations for perishable goods.

## Shipping Rates & Distance Thresholds
- **Free Delivery Radius**: Waived shipping fees (Free Delivery) for orders within a 5 km to 10 km radius from our nursery.
- **Short Distance Delivery (Under 5 km)**: Charged based on distance at a rate of Rs. 10 per km from our nursery.
- **Long Distance Delivery (Over 10 km)**: Charged based on distance at a rate of Rs. 15 per km from our nursery.
- **Express Same-Day Delivery Surcharge**: Additional Rs. 150 flat fee (waived for orders above Rs. 2,499).

## Delivery Process for Live Plants
- **Hydration Pre-Transit**: Plants are thoroughly watered and their root balls are wrapped in moisture-retaining moss before packaging.
- **Unboxing Instructions**: Open the package immediately upon receipt, keep the plant in indirect sunlight for 2 days, and water only if the soil is dry.
- **No-Return Pickup**: Because live plants are highly perishable, we do not require customers to ship damaged plants back for a refund/replacement. We simply send a new one.
