# Pricing Rules

## Retail Pricing & Taxes
- All prices listed in our catalog or store are inclusive of 18% GST (Goods and Services Tax).
- Price of potting soil and organic fertilizers includes 5% GST.
- Prices are fixed for standard retail purchases. However, automatic checkout discounts apply based on order scale and customer loyalty:
  - **Huge Purchase Discount**: Get 10% off automatically on orders of Rs. 1,500 or more, or when purchasing 3 or more plants.
  - **Permanent Customer Discount**: Loyal customers who have placed more than 5 completed orders receive a special 15% discount on all purchases.
  - These discounts are stacked and applied automatically by the AI chatbot during checkout.

## Wholesale & Bulk Pricing Rules
- **Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ) for Wholesale**: 
  - Live Plants: Minimum 25 units per cultivar, or a total order value of Rs. 15,000.
  - Gardening tools/pots: Minimum 50 units.
- **Wholesale Discounts**:
  - Orders between Rs. 15,000 and Rs. 50,000: Flat 20% off retail pricing.
  - Orders above Rs. 50,000: Flat 30% off retail pricing.
- **Wholesale Contact**: For inquiries, email wholesale@greenhavennursery.in with your business registry/GST certificate.
